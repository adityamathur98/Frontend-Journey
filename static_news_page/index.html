iB Cricket announces world's first Virtual Reality Cricket League

iB Cricket gives fans an opportunity to play cricket in world-class stadiums in front of packed houses. Very lively and full of energy! It's like playing cricket in international stadiums as the crowd cheers you on. The line and length of the ball was very accurate. Includes player vs player, co-op mode, campaign mode, VR bowling, tournaments, net practice, and coaching
